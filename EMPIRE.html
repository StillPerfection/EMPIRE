About 

Bakuchiol is natures alternative to Retinol (a popular anti wrinkle ingredient). Being an extract derived from the leaves and seeds of the babchi plant, it is a natural element with no known side affects.

Bakuchiol is known for its ability to stimulate skin cell turnover and collagen production, it deeply penetrates the skin to help lessen the appearance of dark spots and discoloration, smooths fine lines and wrinkles and improves skin elasticity and firmness.

We have combined this natural antiaging hero with another of natures miracles, Cacay Oil. Derived from the unique three kernel nut of the Cacay tree, it harbours all the goodness of other anti-ageing ‘miracle’ oils – but in even higher quantities.

Bosting more Vitamin A than Rosehip oil, twice as much Vitamin E & Linoeic Acid than Argan oil, Cacay oil helps to activate the skin's natural renewal process, rebuilding and replenishing skin tissue, visibly reducing the appearance of wrinkles, scars, blemishes and stretch marks.

To top it off we have added Camellia oil for its minerals and ability to stimulate collagen production, Rosehip oil to fight free radical damage and reduce the signs of aging, Chamomile for its powerful anti-inflammatory properties, soothing and healing ability’s, with Argan Oil to lock in the moisture and help protect from the UV.

Usage

Using 3-4 drops, apply to forehead and cheeks and gently rub into skin, once to twice a day (or after washing face). You may also apply to neck and décolletage or the back of your hands.
